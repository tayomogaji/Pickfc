Pick Fc: Rules

Picks

For each round in a competition, you must pick the team you believe will WIN their fixture. Only teams with fixtures can be picked and each team can only be picked ONCE. Failure to make your pick before a round deadline will result in you loosing that round.


Points

Points are awarded on a unique pick basis, meaning, in a given round, 1 point is awarded for every player still with life in the game, who DID NOT pick the same team as you. This is only valid if the team you picked WINS. Zero points are awarded for losses or draws.

Life

All players begin the game with 2 life points. Additional life points are awarded after every 4th consecutive win until its maximum (3) is reached. 1 life point is deducted if your picked team LOOSES their fixture and 2 life points are deducted if your picked team fails to win while you have a hit placed on you.

End Game

The game ends once all players are eliminated or when all rounds for a competition are complete. The player with most points at the end of the game, wins.


Pick Time (Tie break)

Measured in DAYS, the time taken for you to make your final pick in a given round is calculated and added towards your overall pick time. Should the game end level on points, the player with the quickest pick time wins.
